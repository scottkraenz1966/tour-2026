Dual Battery Strategy — 2x 983Wh

⚡ 2x 983Wh = ~1,966Wh total · ~140–160km combined realistic range

Daily Routine

  • Start every day on B1 fully charged. B2 also fully charged and carried on the rack.
  • Swap trigger: When B1 hits ~20–25% remaining (not zero — buffer for headwinds, detours).
  • Stage 3 exception: Swap earlier (~55–60km) due to the 542m elevation load on the motor.
  • Use Recup on every descent — especially the Wachau Valley (Stage 4) and Danube Bend (Stage 8). Set it to a firm strength level, not just light braking.

Charging

  • 5.5h per battery to full. If you only have one charger, do B1 first (arrives more depleted), then B2 overnight.
  • Carry a short extension lead — guesthouses often have awkward socket placement relative to bike storage.
  • On the rest day in Linz (Day 4), charge both during the day — you have 10+ hours available. Best opportunity of the trip.
  • In Vienna and Budapest, confirm the hotel has a secure room or corridor you can leave the bike in with the charger running.
