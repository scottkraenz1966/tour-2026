Things that will bite if you forget them
  • Day 3 is the hard one (119 km, 607 m). It sits directly before the Linz rest day, which is the right place for it, but do not start it late.
  • Trstena na Ostrove has four rooms. That is the single biggest risk on the route. Either book far ahead or plan to stop at Samorin instead.
  • Kronau is not a stop. Your day-5 track ends there and there is nowhere to sleep. Tulln is 4 km further.
  • Map the Labatlan to Budapest leg in Komoot before departure, and download every stage offline.
  • Hungary is not on the euro. Carry some forints for the last three days.
  • Two border crossings on the bike: Germany to Austria at Passau on day 3, Austria to Slovakia to Hungary across days 7 and 8.
  • Ask every hotel two questions when booking: lockable indoor bike space overnight, and late check-in.
